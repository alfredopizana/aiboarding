# Product Team Playbook

## How we plan

Quarterly roadmap planning happens in the last two weeks of each quarter.
Each initiative needs a one-page spec: problem, evidence, proposal, metrics.

## Spec writing

Specs live in Confluence under "Product / Specs". Use the template.
Every spec is reviewed async by engineering and design before kickoff.

## Metrics

North-star metric: weekly active workspaces. Dashboards live in Looker.
Every PM owns a metrics dashboard reviewed in the Monday sync.

## Customer research

We run continuous discovery: at least 3 customer interviews per week per squad.
Recordings and synthesis notes go in the research repository in Notion.
Talk to Sofía Ruiz to join interview shadowing rotations.
