# Company Handbook (excerpt)

## Mission

We help teams ship software faster with less friction.

## Organization

- Platform team: infrastructure, deployments, reliability.
- Web team: customer-facing product.
- Product team: strategy, roadmap, customer research.
- Security team: compliance, access control, training.
- People team: onboarding, benefits, growth.

## First week checklist

1. Complete HR paperwork in Workday.
2. Finish security training (mandatory in the first 5 days).
3. Set up your accounts: Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub, Confluence.
4. Meet your onboarding buddy — they are your first point of contact.
5. Read your team's charter page in Confluence.

## Benefits and time off

Unlimited PTO with a 15-day minimum. Health insurance from day one.
Ask People Ops (Ana Torres) about payroll, benefits enrollment, or the handbook.

## Communication norms

Default to async and public channels. Document decisions in Confluence.
Use Slack for quick questions, email for external communication.
