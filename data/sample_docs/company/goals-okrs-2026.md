# Company Goals & OKRs — 2026

Owner: Leadership (draft: Sofía Ruiz, @sofia). Cadence: yearly objectives, quarterly key results.
Tracking: OKR board in Notion ("Company / OKRs 2026"), scored 0.0–1.0 monthly; 0.7 is a good landing.

## How we write OKRs here

- Objectives are qualitative and memorable; key results are measurable and *outcomes*, not tasks.
- 3 objectives per level, max 4 KRs each. If everything is a priority, nothing is.
- Team OKRs ladder up to company OKRs — every team KR names the company KR it serves.
- Committed KRs (marked ★) are promises; aspirational KRs are ambition. Don't mix them up in planning.

## Company objectives 2026

### O1 — Make onboarding the product's front door

New users reach value in their first session, not their first month.

- KR1 ★ Median time-to-first-answer for new workspaces < 10 minutes (from 47 min, Dec 2025).
- KR2 Week-4 retention of new workspaces ≥ 55% (from 41%).
- KR3 90% of new users complete the guided setup without a support touch.

### O2 — Earn the enterprise

Security, reliability, and admin controls that let a 5,000-seat company say yes.

- KR1 ★ SOC 2 Type II renewed with zero major findings (audit window closes Q3).
- KR2 ★ Availability ≥ 99.9% every month, publicly reported on the status page.
- KR3 SSO/SCIM shipped GA + adopted by 20 enterprise workspaces.
- KR4 p95 API latency < 300 ms at 2× current peak load.

### O3 — Grow efficiently

Revenue grows faster than costs, and we can prove which bets pay.

- KR1 Net revenue retention ≥ 115%.
- KR2 Infrastructure cost per active workspace down 20% (baseline: Jan 2026 unit-cost dashboard).
- KR3 Every growth experiment ships with a pre-registered success metric (tracked by Data team).

## Quarterly rhythm

- **Week 1 of quarter**: teams publish OKRs, leadership resolves collisions in one planning review.
- **Monthly**: scores updated before the all-hands; reds get a one-line "what changes" note, not a defense.
- **Last week of quarter**: retro + scoring. Sandbagging (all 1.0s) is treated the same as all-reds: recalibrate.
