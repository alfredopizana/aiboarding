# Product Roadmap — 2026

Owner: Product (Sofía Ruiz, @sofia). Reviewed monthly at the roadmap review; this file mirrors
the live board in Notion ("Product / Roadmap"). Dates are quarters, not promises —
we communicate specific dates only when a thing reaches "Now".

## How to read this roadmap

**Now / Next / Later**, one page. "Now" is committed and staffed. "Next" is scoped and likely.
"Later" is direction, not commitment — it exists so customers and teams can see where we're heading.
Anything not on the page is a "no, for now" (see the parking lot at the bottom of the live board).

## Now (Q3 2026)

- **Guided workspace setup v2** — serves O1/KR3. Checklist-driven first-run, template gallery,
  success metric: setup completion without support touch.
- **SSO + SCIM GA** — serves O2/KR3. Okta and Entra certified; admin audit log ships with it.
- **Answer citations v2** — inline source previews; groundwork for the trust work in "Next".
- **Unit-cost dashboard** — serves O3/KR2; per-workspace infra cost visible to every team lead.

## Next (Q4 2026)

- **Answer feedback loop** — thumbs + corrections feed retrieval quality reviews weekly.
- **Enterprise roles & permissions** — custom roles, workspace-level policies.
- **EU data residency** — Frankfurt region; pairs with the Q4 compliance audit window.

## Later (2027 direction)

- Multi-language answers (ES and PT first — half our new hires read Spanish natively).
- Knowledge freshness scoring: stale-doc detection feeding owner nudges.
- Public API v2 with webhooks GA (drafts live in `company/architecture/adr/`).

## How things get on the roadmap

1. Anyone files an opportunity in the product tracker with evidence (support themes, sales notes, data).
2. Product triages weekly; opportunities that survive get a one-page brief (problem, evidence, sizing).
3. Quarterly planning ranks briefs against OKRs — capacity comes from the team, not wishful math.
4. Post-ship, every roadmap item gets a 30-day outcome check against its success metric —
   shipped-but-didn't-move-the-metric goes back to discovery, not quietly into the win column.
